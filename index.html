What were are going?

Platform

Our online educational platform, omegaUp.com, provides free high quality content in Spanish to underserved segments of the population, including indigenous and rural communities. In 2020, we served more than 26,000 users throughout Mexico, the USA, Peru, Argentina, Colombia, Ecuador, Bolivia, El Salvador, and other LATAM countries including more than 300 schools that used omegaUp for their CS Courses.

Contests

omegaUp has been adopted as the ocial platform for the Olympiad of Informatics in Mexico, Ecuador and Peru.

Contests include Coding Cup ITSUR, Coding Cup TecNM, Coding Cup Michoacán, Coding Cup Tamaulipas, and Concurso de Tecnológicos de Veracruz.

Clubs

This is an opportunity for teachers and students who want to expand beyond the classroom to competitive programming. We provide training, support, and a mentor to help them.

Courses

Since last year we have been launching online courses for:

Students who want to learn C++: Our approach is to teach through experiential learning with the help of our platform, so immediately after students finish a lesson they start solving programming challenges.

Teachers who teach introductory courses: We developed an educational model to help teachers adapt our platforms to the classroom. omegaUp provides training, support and educational material teachers
can use with their students.
